# Hypt Cheatsheet

> Your quick reference for working with your AI coding assistant.
> Save or print this page so you always have it handy.
>
> **hypt v0.23.0**

---

## The 4 Commands You'll Use Most

### Ship something (fully automatic)
**Command:** `/yolo`
**Or just say:** *"ship it"*, *"just ship it"*, *"yolo"*

Give it a bug to fix, a feature idea, or a change you want — and it handles everything automatically. It writes the code, reviews it, tests it, and puts it live on your site. You don't need to do anything else.

---

### Ship something (with your approval first)
**Command:** `/go`
**Or just say:** *"go"*, *"go mode"*, *"ship with confirmation"*

Same as above, but it pauses right before putting changes live and shows you a summary. You say "yes" to approve, or "no" to ask for changes. Great when you want to double-check before it goes live.

---

### Save an idea for later
**Command:** `/todo`
**Or just say:** *"add to my backlog"*, *"track this"*, *"remember this idea"*

Brain dump any thought, idea, bug, or feature request. It gets saved, categorized, and prioritized automatically. Come back to it whenever you're ready.

---

### What should I do next?
**Command:** `/suggestions`
**Or just say:** *"what should I work on next?"*, *"what's next?"*, *"suggestions"*

Looks at your project and tells you the most impactful thing to work on. Pick one and it can start building it right away.

---

## Other Handy Commands

| What you want | Command | Or just say... |
|---|---|---|
| Save your work | `/save` | *"save"*, *"commit"*, *"push"* |
| Check if your site is up | `/status` | *"is my site up?"*, *"status"* |
| Fix a bug | `/fix` | *"fix this"*, *"something's broken"*, *"not working"* |
| Roll back a bad change | `/restore` | *"restore"*, *"rollback"*, *"undo"* |
| Update your docs | `/docs` | *"update docs"*, *"refresh docs"* |
| Start a brand new project | `/start` | *"I have an idea"*, *"get started"* |

---

## Tips

- **You don't need to memorize commands.** Just describe what you want in plain English and your assistant will figure it out.
- **Start simple.** `/yolo` and `/todo` cover 90% of what you'll need day-to-day.
- **Everything is reversible.** If something goes wrong, say *"restore"* to roll back to a working version.
- **Your site updates automatically.** When you ship a change, it goes live within a minute or two.
